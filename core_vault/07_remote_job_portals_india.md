# Remote Job Portals For India-Compatible Software Roles

Updated: 2026-04-10

Purpose: Prioritize high-signal AI/Founding roles while maintaining a high-volume automated engine.

## Tier 1: High-Signal, High-Touch (Founding Engineer Targets)
*Strategy: Scout & Draft. Manual review and submission required.*

1. **YC Work at a Startup**
   - **Portal:** https://www.workatastartup.com/
   - **Focus:** Early-stage AI, RAG, and Founding Engineer roles.
2. **Wellfound (formerly AngelList)**
   - **Portal:** https://wellfound.com/jobs
   - **Focus:** Hungry AI startups looking for MERN/TypeScript product engineers.
3. **Direct ATS Boards (Ashby, Greenhouse, Lever)**
   - **Portal:** site:jobs.ashbyhq.com, site:boards.greenhouse.io
   - **Focus:** Top-tier AI companies (Sarvam AI, Signzy, Groq).

## Tier 2: The Volume Engine (Automated via Agent)
*Strategy: Fully Auto-Apply workflows using standard forms.*

1. **Cutshort**
   - **Portal:** https://cutshort.io/
   - **Focus:** India-first product and startup roles (AI/MERN).
2. **Instahyre**
   - **Portal:** https://www.instahyre.com/
   - **Focus:** 1-click apply for Indian tech product companies and fintechs.
3. **LinkedIn ("Easy Apply" Only)**
   - **Portal:** https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/
   - **Focus:** Aggressive filtering for "Easy Apply" on "AI Engineer" and "RAG Pipelines."

## Tier 3: The Global Remote Scrape
*Strategy: Background monitoring and exact-match Auto-Apply.*

1. **Arc.dev**
   - **Portal:** https://arc.dev/en-in/remote-jobs
   - **Focus:** Verified global remote roles explicitly open to India.
2. **Himalayas.app**
   - **Portal:** https://himalayas.app/
3. **Remotive**
   - **Portal:** https://remotive.com/

## Weekly Workflow
1. **Daily (AM):** Trigger Tier 2 Auto-Apply engine (Cutshort, Instahyre).
2. **Daily (PM):** Review Tier 1 "Drafts" (YC, Wellfound) and submit manually.
3. **Continuous:** Tier 3 background monitoring for exact stack matches.
