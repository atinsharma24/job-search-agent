# Daily Application Roadmap (FAANG & Big Tech Referral Edition)

## Daily Objective

* Source, verify, and extract active early-career software engineering roles at FAANG, FAANG-adjacent, and Tier-1 mid-tech product companies.


* Isolate exact **Job IDs/Requisition IDs** and direct application URLs to package cleanly for internal network referrals.


* Maintain a balanced pipeline targeting high-growth enterprise SaaS, fintech giants, and trillion-dollar tech ecosystems.



## Non-Negotiable Rules

*  referral friction reduction: Never send a raw aggregator or third-party job board link to a referral contact. Trace the role back to the official company career portal to confirm it is live and extract the precise internal identifier.


* **Experience Gating:** Strictly filter for early-career tracks, University/New Grad cohorts (2025/2026), or Software Engineer I roles. Bypass any listing demanding $3+$ years of experience or senior-level architecture ownership.


* **Stack Alignment:** Prioritize full-stack capabilities, modern web development, and AI engineering spaces (TypeScript, Node.js, Next.js, Python, RAG pipelines, and database optimization).


* **Domain Gating:** Exclude positions centered on low-level Linux operating systems administration, kernel development, or hardware systems engineering.


* **Core Metrics Graining:** Keep all automated or semi-automated screening variables locked exactly to the validated profile standards: Notice Period = Immediate (0 days), Expected Compensation = 15–18 LPA, Total Professional Experience = ~1 year.

## Source Priority Architecture

### 1. Daily Open-Source & Community Aggregators (The Velocity Layer)

* **SimplifyJobs GitHub (New Grad Repositories):** Check daily for newly committed 2025/2026 early-career listings across top-tier organizations.


* **TrueUp.io & Levels.fyi/jobs:** Use these tech-specific indexes to check for real-time hiring velocity, filtering strictly by tech stack and early-career bands.



### 2. Direct Portal Verification (The Referral Layer)

* **The FAANG Cluster:** Directly audit active listings across Meta, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, and Google portals.


* **High-Growth Fintech & Enterprise SaaS:** Audit dedicated tracking pipelines for Stripe, Intuit, PayPal, Salesforce, Databricks, and Snowflake.


* **Developer Infrastructure Tools:** Monitor Vercel, GitHub, and major cloud/infrastructure providers.

## Daily Sourcing Time Blocks

### Block 1: Discovery & Scrape (45 Minutes)

* **Action:** Run your automated Cowork execution layer across the community repositories and platform trackers (SimplifyJobs, TrueUp, Levels.fyi).


* **Focus:** Isolate new entries under the keywords: *Software Engineer I, Associate Engineer, Full Stack Developer, AI Engineer, Backend Engineer*.


* **Output:** A raw, multi-company list of unverified early-career openings matching your core languages (TypeScript/Python).



### Block 2: Portal Verification & Data Extraction (45 Minutes)

* **Action:** Take the raw list and systematically open the direct company career pages.


* **Verification Checklist:**
1. Is the job location open to India (Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, NCR, Remote)?


2. Is the application hosted directly on an internal ATS (Workday, Greenhouse, internal tools) rather than an agency portal?


3. Locate and copy the exact **Requisition ID / Job ID** (typically found in the header or footer metadata).




* **Output:** Clean, vetted data rows populated in your tracking system.



### Block 3: Referral Packaging (30 Minutes)

* **Action:** Group verified opportunities by company and referral connection.


* **Format:** Package the final link list explicitly with:
* Target Role Title + Company


* Exact Requisition ID


* Direct application URL


* A single-sentence bullet point mapping your specific project history (e.g., building production RAG pipelines, optimizing database latencies) to that team’s focus area.

## Resume Routing Rules

### Category A: GenAI, RAG, & Conversational Focus

* **Trigger Words:** *LLM, RAG, embeddings, semantic search, pgvector, prompt engineering, vector databases, agents*.


* **Action:** Align the sourcing packet with technical highlights detailing your zero-to-one RAG builds, streaming API implementations, and multi-model failover architectures.



### Category B: Tier-1 Full-Stack & Core Backend Systems

* **Trigger Words:** *MERN stack, TypeScript, Next.js, API design, PostgreSQL, query optimization, high-throughput web architectures*.


* **Action:** Focus application data points on database latency reductions (e.g., fixing N+1 query loops), schema design, billing integration robustness, and microservice containerization.

## End-of-Day Operations & Cleanup

1. Verify all newly discovered targets are appended to your active tracking schema.


2. Clear temporary browser tabs and download caches to maintain workflow speed on your system.


3. Flag roles with high-priority internal network connections for immediate, prioritized outreach before the requisition pool closes.
